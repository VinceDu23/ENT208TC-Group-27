基于 COCO 17 关键点人体模型，实时计算髋、膝、肩、肘、踝、躯干 6 大关节角度。640×480 输入下达到 30fps 实时推理。Based on the COCO 17-keypoint human model, computes hip, knee, shoulder, elbow, ankle, and torso angles in real time. Delivers 30 fps inference at 640×480 input resolution.

杠铃轨迹追踪Barbell Trajectory Tracking

每 reps 实时绘制杠铃运动路径，与理想轨迹可视化对比。自动计算偏移量（cm），深蹲前移、硬拉远离身体等错误即时检测。Plots the barbell path for every rep in real time, visually compared against the ideal trajectory. Automatically calculates deviation (cm) to instantly detect forward lean in squats, bar drift in deadlifts, and more.

Reads heart rate (HR) and IMU accelerometer data in real time. 5-zone HR visualization + calorie estimation + recovery monitoring.

工作流程How It Works

4 步开启智能训练4 Steps to Start Training Smarter

1

启动后端服务Launch the Backend

终端运行 python pose_server.py，加载 YOLOv8 模型并打开摄像头，WebSocket 服务就绪。Run python pose_server.py in your terminal — it loads the YOLOv8 model, opens your camera, and the WebSocket server is ready.

→
2

打开网页应用Open the Web App

浏览器访问 localhost，登录后进入仪表盘。支持 Chrome / Edge，中英双语界面。Navigate to localhost in your browser, log in, and enter the dashboard. Works in Chrome and Edge with a bilingual (ZH/EN) interface.

→
3

连接设备Connect Your Devices

一键连接 YOLO 姿态服务 + BLE 心率带 / 加速度计。系统自动融合多源数据，也可在模拟模式下离线演示。One-click connect to the YOLO pose service and your BLE heart rate strap or accelerometer. The system auto-fuses multi-source data — or use simulation mode for an offline demo.

→
4

开始训练Start Training

选择动作（深蹲/卧推/硬拉/肩推），点击开始。实时查看关节点角度、杠铃轨迹、心率区间，姿势异常即时报警。Pick an exercise (Squat / Bench Press / Deadlift / Overhead Press) and hit Start. Watch your joint angles, bar path, and HR zone in real time with instant form alerts.
